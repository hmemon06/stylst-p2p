# 🔄 Auto-Renewal Disclosure Text for Superwall Paywall

## Full Version (Recommended)

Copy this to your Superwall paywall footer:

```
Payment will be charged to your Apple ID account at confirmation of purchase. Subscription automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel your subscription by going to your Account Settings in the App Store after purchase. Any unused portion of a free trial will be forfeited when purchasing a subscription.
```

---

## Short Version (if space is limited)

```
Auto-renews. Cancel anytime in App Store settings at least 24 hours before renewal.
```

---

## Where to Add This

1. Go to **Superwall Dashboard**
2. Open your paywall editor
3. Add a **Text Block** at the bottom (footer section)
4. Paste the text above
5. Style it: Small font (10-12px), gray color (#888)
6. Make sure it's readable but not intrusive

---

## Also Add Links

Below the disclosure, add clickable links:
```
Privacy Policy | Terms of Service
```

Link them to:
- Privacy: `https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/stylst/privacy.html`
- Terms: `https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/stylst/terms.html`

## Apple Requirements Checklist

Your paywall MUST show:
- ✅ Price (e.g., "$6.99/month")
- ✅ Duration (monthly/yearly)
- ✅ Auto-renewal disclosure (text above)
- ✅ Link to Privacy Policy
- ✅ Link to Terms of Service
- ✅ Restore Purchases button (you have this in profile ✅)
